Rivers & Surface Water

  • Chemical coloring and foam covering rivers
  • Oxygen depletion → mass fish kills
  • Disruption of food webs (insects → fish → birds)
  • Toxic algal blooms (some produce cyanotoxins)

Groundwater & Drinking Water

  • Heavy metals and PFAS ("forever chemicals") leach deep
  • Wells become undrinkable for generations
  • Very expensive / almost impossible to remediate
  • Long-distance transport through aquifers
