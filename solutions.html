Most Effective Actions (2025–2026 view)

  1. Zero-liquid discharge systems in high-risk industries
  2. Mandatory real-time effluent monitoring + public data access
  3. Strict limits on PFAS, heavy metals, forever chemicals
  4. Circular economy — reuse water & recover materials inside factories
  5. Stronger international river basin agreements
  6. Support cleaner production technologies (especially small factories)
  7. Consumer pressure → prefer brands with verified clean production

Clean water is still possible — but we must act faster.
