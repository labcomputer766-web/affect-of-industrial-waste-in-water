Our water is under silent attack

Every year industries release millions of tons of chemicals, heavy metals and untreated waste into rivers, lakes and directly into the ground.

Quick Facts

  • ≈ 70–80% of industrial wastewater is released untreated in many developing regions
  • Heavy metals (mercury, lead, cadmium) do not break down — they accumulate
  • Once groundwater is contaminated, cleanup can take decades or centuries
  • Over 1.8 billion people use contaminated water sources (2024–2025 estimates)
